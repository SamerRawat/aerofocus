Get Up and Running in Seconds

Simple NSIS standalone Windows installer gets your workspace optimized instantly.

1

Download the Setup File

Click the Download for Windows button above to download the official standalone NSIS desktop installer package (`Aero Focus Setup 1.0.0.exe`).

2

Accept SmartScreen Prompts

Open the setup file. If Windows Defender SmartScreen pops up warning you, click on "More Info" and select "Run Anyway" to securely complete the installation.

3

Enjoy Your Study Hub

The app will launch and lock directly to your desktop workspace background behind your desktop icons. You are ready to start focusing and tracking your goals!
